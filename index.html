DIY WITH CAUTION

NEED MORE INFO

Why it's different

Not another photo-to-wall-of-text app.

Three things the app-store crowd doesn't do.

01 · DIAGNOSE

Instant photo diagnosis

Point your camera at the problem. An answer in seconds — the likely cause, severity, time and cost — instead of a YouTube rabbit hole and 12 half-matching thumbnails.

02 · GUIDE

Guided Fix Mode

The differentiator. One step at a time, in big readable text — with a progress bar, the tools you need for this step, and a safety check built right into the flow. Follow it one-handed while you work.

03 · TRIAGE

Honest triage

It tells you when not to DIY — and hands you a ready-to-send paragraph describing the issue for your tradesperson, plus links to find one. No pretending everything's fixable.
